About Us

Placement & Training
Gallery

Training and Placement Division of Bishop Heber College has an unmatched record in campus placement . The Division endeavours to provide inputs that will help the students to enhance their employability skills and carve a niche for themselves in the global arena.Intensive Training Programmes and workshopsare designed and conducted by Training and Placement Division to professionalize students in relation to their fields. The Division takes an extra effort to closely monitor the perfomance of the students who have applied for jobs and it also helps them in devising materials and getting ready for interviews and job talks.Crash courses on soft skills are also conducted to fine tune the employability skills of the students. 1417 final year students have benefited by using the facilities offered by Training and Placement Division . 451 students have been placed in prestigious companies such as TCS, CTS,Infosys,Reliance etc in the academic year 2019-2020. Companies such as s TCS, CTS,Infosys,Reliance,Deloitte etc frequently visit heber campus to recruite students. Students are also encouraged to attend off campus interviews.
